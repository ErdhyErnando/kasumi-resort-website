**Kasumi Resort** offers stays among trees and cool mountain air in **Mount Halimun Salak National Park (TNGHS), Cidahu**, Sukabumi. We combine comfortable accommodation with calm nature access — ideal for families, couples, and small groups who want a break from city routines.

## Villas in the forest

Our **villas** are designed for privacy and varied views. Each has its own character — warm interiors and lounge spaces facing forest or valley. Clean bathrooms, reliable electricity, and thoughtful basics help you stay comfortable without losing the outdoor feel.

## Camping and outdoor stays

For guests who want to be closer to the stars and night sounds, our **camping areas** offer different moods — from riverside forest to more open views. We keep the experience managed and welcoming for first-timers and seasoned campers alike.

## Activities and resort facilities

Around the resort you can plan easy walks or more adventurous outings — campfires, trekking, and other options we share when you book. **Wi-Fi**, shared spaces, and guest services help your trip stay practical.

## Planning your visit

Explore the **villa** and **camping** pages on our site for options, facility notes, and **WhatsApp** contact. We are happy to help with dates, packages, and travel notes from Jakarta or Bogor to Cidahu.

Thank you for getting to know Kasumi — we hope to welcome you soon under the morning mist and Sukabumi’s natural soundtrack.
